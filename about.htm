About Us

About The Founder

Designing computers was a hobby that I used to pass my time. Helping others with their builds was such fun that I wanted to create a company that would allow anyone to get top of the line PC's that are cheap, and hand crafted every time. Thus, the idea of TechStar was born. And look at where we are today, giving the gift of gaming to people around the world.

Our Goals

We want to work together with our partners to build you the best possible computers for the most reasonabe price. Alot of thought goes into what we buy to build your computer, and alot of love goes into it when we hand craft it in house for you. Building and shipping to anywhere in the world can take time, but we try to get each computer up and away within three days of receving the parts to build your computer.
